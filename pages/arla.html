ARLAGÅRDEN PLUS

We were given an opportunity to collaborate with Arla's UX team to create a digital platform for their farmers.

MAKING DATA ENTRY MORE APPEALING

Members of Arlagården Plus are bound to fill out a long, repetitive survey each quarter about sustainability on their farms. Our job was to make it easier for them and keep them coming back on a daily basis.

My Role

Researching, planning an intuitive and clean interface, coding the dashboard's main page, together with the calculator, illustrating a cow mascot.

THE CENTER OF OPERATION

Our idea to make all the parts of the website easily and quickly accesible was to create a dashboard. The sections lead to the specific page and some of them can be interacted with. For instance, the calculator helps the users figure out how much money they will earn for their milk production.

THE SURVEY

A crucial part of the application was to make filling out the survey easier. So we thought of a mechanism that lets the users stop at any point and come back later and still know where they left off.

A REASON TO SMILE

It's been proven many times that a company mascot can make a big difference with both brand identity and product interaction. We got inspired by that and developed a cow mascot which accompanies the users throughout the app. It was designed in Adobe Illustrator.
