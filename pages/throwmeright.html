Search for waste items

The most important part of the app was the search function. We put it on the home page so that our users have the easiest access to it possible.

We used Firebase to store our items and then fetched them with JavaScript to compare them to the input's value.

Explore the map

Another crucial piece of functionality was the map with recycling centers. We categorized them by waste type so you can always find the one you're looking for and included a slider which filters through them.

This is the part which I developed on my own. I chose to work with the Mapbox API, where I could style it and add my own locations.
