BASEMENT BUDDY

A digital tablet display designed and developed for a student bar ‘Basement’ at Business Academy Aarhus. It was looking to encourage more students to visit by introducing some form of interactive solution.

It was developed only for tablet-sized screen.

Games, music and events

Our idea was to place a couple of those multi-purpose tablets on tables around the bar for the students to interact with. They would be able to play games, put songs in the queue and read about upcoming events.

My Role

We worked on the structure and mockups together, however I was responsible for coding the home page and the Taboo game.

Drinking made even more fun

The game is based on a math.random JavaScript method. It allows the users to draw random numbers with their friends and whoever gets the lowest one pays for the drinks.

The Battle of Genders

In this game boys and girls can play against each other to see who knows more about the opposite gender. I developed a timer, random cards and a message that pops up after 5 one round.
