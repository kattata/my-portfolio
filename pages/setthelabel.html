SET THE LABEL

An exam project, where my group and I had to find a company to create a digital solution for. We decided to collaborate with Set The Label, which is a fashion start up based in Aarhus, Denmark.

To empower and unite women of all kinds

The company's goal is to help women of all types feel beautiful and confident by releasing an inclusive store and a place to communicate and learn.

Effortless shopping

One of the crucial parts of an online store is the product page. We put all of our efforts into making it intuitive and simple.

I worked with GSAP and ScrollMagic to enable scrolling through the details while having the product images in sight.

Building a trustworthy visual identity

As we found out during numerous interviews with our client, they put a big emphasis on connecting with their user and creating a bond by creating an experience that goes with their brand values: Quality, Simplicity and Community. We had that in mind while designing the layout, choosing colors and icons.
