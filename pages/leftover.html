EXCHANGE LEFT OVER FOOD

Our concept was to give people an opportunity to share their left over food with others in order to reduce food waste as well as educate them on how to change their habits.

BROWSE AND BUY

On the home page the user can browse through products posted by other users, narrow them down by search or filters, see a detailed view and make a purchase.

When buying a product, the user is asked to choose one of the time slots preselected by the seller. Then they are redirected to a payment service (currently not functional!) and view a summary, which they can access in their profile.

We store our data in a database created with MySQL.

SELL YOUR LEFT OVERS

Besides buying food from others, the user can post their own products. They need to enter information and select from a handful of options, which will be displayed in the feed and help other users filter their results.

MANAGE YOUR PROFILE

Because the goal of the app is becoming more environmentally friendly, they also have access to some statistics describing what effects their actions have.

The users also have access to a history of their purchases to make sure they always arrive on time for pick up.

SIGN UP AND LOG IN

The foundation of an application like this was user authentication, which allowed us to display specific data based on the logged in user id.

Both sign up and log in require a certain amount of validation and if it isn't passed, there are error messages displayed to guide the user in the right direction.
