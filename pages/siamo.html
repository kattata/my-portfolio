SIAMO STREET FOOTBALL

An exam project my group and I worked on for a local start up called Siamo Street Football, which specializes in organizing sports events.

TO CONNECT FOOTBALL ENTHUSIASTS

Siamo's goal is to unite and motivate young, active people to do what they love and find like-minded friends. They operate only on Social Media so with this solution we wanted to give them more opportunities.

My Role

Meeting with the founders, research, designing both the user experience and the interface, coding the events page and the gallery.

BOOK AN EVENT

The most important and interactive part of this web app is the Events page. It allows users to browse through upcoming games and tournaments, as well as sign up for them.

We used Wordpress as a Headless CMS together with The Events Calendar plugin. It was crucial to use a CMS for us to allow our client to manage the data independently after deployment.

EXPERIENCE THE GAME ONCE AGAIN WITH THE GALLERY

Another page that was essential for our client. They needed a place to display photos from their past events in an organized way. So we created a gallery with a custom built preview mode.
